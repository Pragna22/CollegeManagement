ESTRELLA PRODUCTION



Human, being the most intelligent and creative creature on Earth, has always worked hard for the betterment of self and society, which would remain unfulfilled if there was no such scope for entertainment.

⇒ ⇒  But then again, what else can be more fascinating than the Motion of Life? This urge of capturing the streaming life with passion, intelligence and emotions along with a silver touch of glamour, gave birth to FILM.

⇒ ⇒  Estrella Productions, the official film making club of Future Institute Of Engineering and Management, provides you with an absolutely gorgeous platform to celebrate LIFE, through creation and celebration of films that promises to take you all though a wonderful journey of emotions, sensibility and imagination.
